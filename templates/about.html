What's new!

We have redesigned our front end to make it more convenient for our users to find the most relevant information towards their repository's health

As you may have notice we added in a new card view to help you better visualize your repository health!

You can toggle between card view and table view using the two buttons on the right of the page.

In card view we have separated the data into small cards to help you visualize the health of your repository.

We have also added in a new metric called change request which details the amount of times a pull request was issued for the repository.
